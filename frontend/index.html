Unsupervised Pipeline Overview

Details about the feature engineering and ML training stages

1

Data Imputation & Cleaning

Fills missing Income records using dataset median. Drops age and income outliers to prevent cluster skewness (Age < 90, Income < $600,000).

2

Feature Engineering

Derives customer age, sign-up tenure in days, total children, and consolidates all product category expenditures into a unified Total_Spending metric.

3

Categorical Encoding & Scaling

One-hot encodes simplified Education and Living_With features. Normalizes numerical features via StandardScaler.

4

Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

Reduces 18-dimensional features to 3 orthogonal Principal Components, preserving variance and optimizing spatial distance calculations.

5

Agglomerative Clustering & KNN Classifier

Trains Agglomerative Hierarchical clustering to isolate 4 customer profiles. Adapts a 1-Nearest Neighbors Classifier to assign cluster segments to new inputs instantly.
